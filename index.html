Maxima-Matrix-Calculus by Mickle-Mouse

Package for advanced manipulation of matrices (and other tensor manipulations)

We are building a package for Maxima for advanced manipulation, simplification, and calculus for matrices. All too often when working with multivariate random variables, network structures (e.g., artificial neural networks), or other matrix intensive problems, Maxima's packages are lacking. Our goal is to deliver a package that provides most (maybe some day all) of the rules/identities in The Matrix Cookbook (orion.uwaterloo.ca/~hwolkowi/matrixcookbook.pdf).
